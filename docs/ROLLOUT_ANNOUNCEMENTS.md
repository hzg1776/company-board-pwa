# Communications and Alert Center Rollout Pack

Use this pack as-is for launch day communication.

## 1. Employee Announcement

### Subject

Communications and Alert Center Is Now Live

### Message

Starting today, our official place for company updates and alerts is the Communications and Alert Center.

Employee access link:

`https://itotexpress.com/palzivalerts/employee`

Please use this link to sign in with your employee credentials and view company notices, updates, and alerts.

If you want faster access on your phone, open the employee link in your mobile browser and add it to your Home Screen.

Important:

- If you installed an older version previously, remove the old Home Screen app and reinstall it from the employee link above.
- Employees should use the employee link only.
- HR, Systems, and IT access is handled separately.

## 3. One-Page Meeting Script

Good [morning/afternoon], everyone.

Starting today, we are using one official place for company updates and alerts: the Communications and Alert Center.

For employees, there is one direct login page:

`https://itotexpress.com/palzivalerts/employee`

That is the page you should use for company notices, alerts, and updates. Most employees do not need any other page.

You can open that link in your browser and sign in with your employee credentials. If you want faster access on your phone, you can add it to your Home Screen after you open it.

If you installed an older version of this app previously, remove the old Home Screen app and reinstall it from the employee link so it opens correctly.

Our HR, Systems and Analytics, and IT access is separate and will continue to be handled internally. Those internal access links are not for general employee use.

From this point forward, please treat the employee link as the official channel for company communications and alerts.

If you have trouble signing in or do not receive alerts after setup, report it right away so we can fix it quickly.

Thank you.

## 4. Presenter Notes

- Show only the employee URL during all-staff communication.
- Do not display admin credentials or admin routes on a shared screen.
- If you do a live demo, use the employee page only.
- If asked where admins go, answer: "That access is handled separately by the internal team."
